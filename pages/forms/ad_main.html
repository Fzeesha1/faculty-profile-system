Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Home
  2. Faqs

WHERE IS NAMAL COLLEGE? ARE THERE MULTIPLE CAMPUSES IN PAKISTAN?

There is only one campus of Namal College, which is located in Mianwali District, 30 KM from Mianwali city on Talagang Road. It is 90 minute drive from motorway through Balkasar Interchange near Chakwal city.

WHAT PROGRAMS ARE OFFERED AT NAMAL COLLEGE AND WHAT IS THEIR DURATION?

There are three 4-year undergraduate degree programs being offered at Namal College, i.e. BSc. Electrical Engineering, BSc. Computer Science and BBA.

WHICH DEGREE WILL I BE AWARDED UPON GRADUATION FROM NAMAL COLLEGE?

Namal College is now affiliated with the university of engineering & technology, Lahore and thereby the degree will also be awarded by the UET, Lahore.

HOW DO I APPLY?

You can apply by filling the online Admission Application at our website www.namal.edu.pk

IS THERE ANY APPLICATION FEE? HOW I HAVE TO PAY IT?

Yes there is admission application fee. You have to pay a non-refundable admission application fee to be deposited at any branch of Habib Bank Limited in Pakistan. Please deposit PKR: 500 for applying into any/all the three programs.
Once you have filled and submitted the online application, you will get a notification via email on your given e-mail address mentioning two things:
 ▦  Application Acceptance
 ▦  Link for attachment of scanned copy of the fee submitted bank challan form
Once you have submitted the application form, just download the challan form from the website, get a print and deposit the fee at any Branch of HBL. Afterwards, attach & send a scanned soft copy of this bank challan through the link mentioned in point 2 above.
Without attaching scanned copy of the challan form, your application will not be considered for admission.

WHAT IS THE FEE STRUCTURE?

The detail of the fee is available on the College website. The College reserves the right to amend fee structure with prior notification to the successful candidates. Also, tuition fee is subject to revision every academic year.

IS THERE ANY FINANCIAL SUPPORT / SCHOLARSHIP AVAILABLE?

Yes, Namal provides merit & need-based financial support & scholarships. However, applications will be solicited after admission process.

WILL THERE BE ANY ENTRY TEST?

There will be no entry test exclusively conducted by Namal College this year. For details, please see the 'Eligibility Criteria' section.

HOW MANY SEATS ARE AVAILABLE FOR EACH PROGRAMME?

There are at least 40 seats allocated for each program of BSc. Electrical Engineering, BSc. Computer Science and BBA.

ARE THERE ANY QUOTA SEATS IN NAMAL COLLEGE?

No. Admission is strictly on merit basis, without any bias of gender, race, religion, or ethnicity.

IS THERE CO-EDUCATION IN NAMAL COLLEGE?

Yes. Namal College has the distinction of being the first professional institution in the district that accords equal learning opportunities to both males and females.

IS HOSTEL ACCOMMODATION AVAILABLE?

Yes. There are separate hostels for males and females. They are situated a short drive away from the College, and students commute on College buses.

IS NAMAL COLLEGE RECOGNIZED BY HEC & PEC?

Yes. Namal College is a recognized institute by Higher Education Commission of Pakistan and Pakistan Engineering Council. All the formal controls, policies & standards of our academic programs, including admissions, as enshrined in the constitution of UET, Lahore will be applicable to academic year of 2017 onwards.
